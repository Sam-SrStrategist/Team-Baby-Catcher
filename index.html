08 · HIPAA Compliance

Healthcare-Grade Security. From Day One.

A pregnancy app built by an OB/GYN must meet the same compliance standards as the practice itself. Every feature is designed with HIPAA at the foundation, not bolted on after launch.

Built-In Compliance Architecture

🔐
End-to-End Encryption All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Symptom logs, health trackers, and personal information never stored in plaintext.
🛡️
BAA-Ready Infrastructure Hosting on HIPAA-eligible cloud services (AWS/GCP) with Business Associate Agreements in place. Audit-ready from day one.
🔑
Access Controls & Authentication Role-based access, multi-factor authentication, and session management. Only the patient sees her data. Admin access is logged and auditable.
📋
Audit Logging Every access to Protected Health Information (PHI) is logged with timestamps, user IDs, and action types. Complete audit trail for compliance reviews.
🗑️
Data Retention & Deletion Users can request data export or deletion at any time. Retention policies are transparent, documented, and compliant with federal requirements.
📱
Secure Mobile Architecture No PHI stored locally on device. Session timeouts, biometric lock, and secure API communication. App Store privacy disclosures and consent flows built in.
